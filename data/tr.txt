1. lower case to upper case

$ tr 'a-z' 'A-Z' <<<"hello world"
HELLO WORLD


2. delete characters

$ tr -d ' \t' <<<"hello world"
helloworld


3. squeeze repeats (replace each input sequence of a repeated character with a single occurrence)

$ tr -s 'a' <<<"aaabbaa"
abba

$ echo -e "aaa\n\n\nbbb" | tr -s '\n'
aaa
bbb
